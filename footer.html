Support

  • Whether you're having trouble unlocking a bike, need help with your account, or just have a question about how our service works — we’ve got you covered.
  • Contact Us – Can’t find what you’re looking for? Reach out via our contact form or email us at support@onbike.co.uk.. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

  • Emergency Support – If you're experiencing an issue during your ride, call our helpline at 8 800 123 45 67. Available 24/7.

    • Terms & Conditions

    Eligibility

  • You must be at least 18 years old to register and use OnBike. By signing up, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and complete.

    • Acount Registration

    To use OnBike, you must create an account via our mobile app or website. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and all activities under your account.

    Using the Bikes

  • Bikes must be used in accordance with local traffic laws and cycling regulations.
  • Riders must inspect the bike before each use and report any issues via the app.
  • You are responsible for the bike during the rental period.
  • Helmets are strongly recommended for your safety but are not provided.

    • Rental & Fees

  • Fees are based on usage time and will be clearly displayed before each ride.
  • Charges are automatically applied to your registered payment method.
  • Additional fees may apply for damage, improper parking, or loss.

    • Paking rules

  • Bikes must be parked at designated docking stations.

    • Privacy Policy

    We collect basic personal and location data to provide and improve our bike-sharing service. Your information is kept secure, never sold, and only shared with trusted partners when necessary (e.g. for payments or support). You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time.
