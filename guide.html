How to ride OnBike?

Step1:

Log in or sign up on the OnBike app or website.

Step2:

Use the map to locate a nearby bike.

Step3:

Enter the bike code to unlock it.

Step4:

Enjoy your ride, follow traffic rules, and stay safe!

Step5:

Park the bike at a nearby dock station and lock it securely.
