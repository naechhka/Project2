How to ride OnBike?

1. Log in or sign up on the OnBike app or website.

2. Use the map to locate a nearby bike.

3. Enter the bike code to unlock it.

4. Enjoy your ride, follow traffic rules, and stay safe!

5. Park the bike at a nearby dock station and lock it securely.
